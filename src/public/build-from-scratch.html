The way
we do things

  • 1
    Discovery
    We identify your project requirements, the scope, and deliverables. Then, we plan the team size and structure.
  • 2
    Design and planning
    After all the details are settled, we get down to designing wireframes, validating and planning the deliverables.
  • 3
    Development and testing
    We set up a team to work on your project using the Agile approach, constantly demoing the results to you.
  • 4
    Launch
    Once the project is complete and tested, we launch it, transfer code ownership to the client and provide 1-month support.
  • 5
    Support
    Identifying and addressing the issues discovered post-launch
